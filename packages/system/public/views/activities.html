Attractions & Activities

Here are a few well known tourist attractions in San Francisco if you find yourself with extra time on your hands:

  • Walk or ride a bus across the Golden Gate Bridge
  • Take in the view from the top of Coit Tower (walk the Filbert Steps too!)
  • Blend in with the rest of the tourists at Pier 39 & Fisherman's Wharf
  • Root for the home team, go watch the San Francisco Giants at AT&T park
  • Escape from the mainland and tour Alcatraz (Book Early!)

Are you in town for longer or a local looking for something to do? Try some of our favorite spots!

  • Check out where Patrick proposed and hike Land's End Trail
  • Experience science at the Exploratorium
  • Experience even more science at the California Academy of Sciences
  • Ride bikes on Angel Island
  • Visit the Walt Disney Family Museum in the Presidio
  • Go see a play or musical
  • Ride the train at San Francisco Zoo
