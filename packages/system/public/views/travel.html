Flying in from out of town or out of country?

  San Francisco can be reached via public transportation from all 3 of the international airports servicing the Bay Area: San Francisco (SFO), Oakland (OAK), and San Jose (SJC). The closest airport is SFO from which you can take BART to downtown. You can also take BART from OAK. From SJC, you can take a bus to Caltrain, but this option will take longer than the other two airports.

Your hotel may also offer a shuttle service from the airport, so make sure to enquire.
